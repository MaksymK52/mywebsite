About us

At CafeScout Haarlem, we make it simple to explore the best cafes and restaurants across the city — from quiet corners for study sessions to lively brunch spots.

Our platform highlights both local favorites and hidden gems, including vegan and vegetarian options, with details on each cafe's menu, atmosphere, and opening hours.

You can read reviews, share your own experiences, and even save your favorite spots for later. Whether you're visiting Haarlem or a local searching for a new coffee ritual, we help you find places that fit your mood, taste, and budget.

HaarlemCafes brings together comfort, community, and good coffee — so every cup feels like home.
